# About

He works with [Dr. Katherine Bowers](https://cenes.ubc.ca/profile/katherine-bowers/) and [Dr. Kate Holland](https://www.slavic.utoronto.ca/people/directories/all-faculty/kate-holland) on the Digital Humanities project _[Computational Dostoevsky.]_(https://github.com/Digital-Dostoevsky)
